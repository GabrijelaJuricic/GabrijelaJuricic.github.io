About

I'm a Cultural Manager and Producer by profession, but, as usual, a silly thing called life changed my plans and made me think again about my career. In the middle of the pandemic, I realised I have (unintentionally) learned a lot about the IT project lifecycle only by listening to my programmer husband having online meetings in our small apartment every day for more than a year and so it hit me - I wanted to be part of it too!

Junior Frontend Web Developer

Until now I have learned the basics (and maybe a bit more than that) of the most relevant skills for a Junior Frontend Web Developer. The next step is to expand my knowledge in a team of experienced developers who could teach me the best practices and help me grow on my way of becoming a great Web Developer and, hopefully, at some point I'll be able to do the same for new developers as well.

Learning programming was challenging and it still is but, hey, I made it this far and I absolutely love it!
